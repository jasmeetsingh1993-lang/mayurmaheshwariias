About the Administrator

Mayur Maheshwari is an accomplished Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer of the 2003 Batch (UP Cadre) with over two decades of distinguished service in strategic governance, large-scale infrastructure development, and economic policy formulation.

Currently serving as the Managing Director of Uttar Pradesh Power Transmission Corporation Limited (UPPTCL) and UP Rajya Vidyut Utpadan Nigam Limited (UPRVUNL), he is spearheading the modernization of the state's power infrastructure. Previously, as CEO of UPSIDA, he curated a land bank of 70,000+ acres and attracted nearly ₹3.5 Lakh Crore in investment, fundamentally reshaping the industrial landscape of Uttar Pradesh.

His tenure at the Prime Minister’s Office (PMO) as Deputy Secretary and Director was marked by critical policy interventions, including the 100% Neem Coated Urea Policy, the launch of the BHIM App, and the Government e-Marketplace (GeM). A technocrat at heart (B.Tech, Textile Technology), he combines administrative depth with technological foresight to deliver "Governance Reimagined". His current focus is on building robust infrastructure to support Uttar Pradesh's vision of becoming a Trillion Dollar Economy.
